Atico Vivo | Interface Design


Ático Vivo is an interactive visualization project that explores how the Ático Center can be interpreted and activated as a living ecosystem of knowledge, infrastructure, and creation. The project articulates data on spaces, laboratories, technologies, professionals, and projects to reveal the relationships that enable audiovisual, digital, and creative production within the university.

By integrating and structuring heterogeneous databases, Ático Vivo constructs a network-based visual interface, where each node represents a component of the ecosystem and each connection reveals collaborations, workflows, and potential intersections between technical and human capabilities. Instead of a static or institutional representation, the system proposes a dynamic and relational interpretation of the Ático Center.

Visualization functions as a tool for exploration and projection: it allows us to understand what already exists, identify patterns of use and collaboration, and at the same time imagine new scenarios for projects, services and research based on non-obvious combinations between laboratories, equipment and people.
