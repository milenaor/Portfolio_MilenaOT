KADE | Immersive exhibition


I was part of the team that conceptualized and carried out the immersive exhibition KADE – Chronicle of a Journey in the Amazon, created as a launch device for a documentary and a multiplatform project that tell the story of a Uitoto-Jesuit indigenous man, developed in La Chorrera, Amazonas.

The exhibition was conceived as a spatial journey through different moments, where each environment sensorially evoked the Amazonian territory and certain narrative threads of the story. Through visual, auditory, and spatial atmospheres, the installation sought to generate in the public the sensation of being in the Amazon, activating an experience prior to the audiovisual work.

The tour culminated with a screening of the documentary produced at the Ático Center, integrating the immersive experience with the film and expanding its interpretive possibilities. In this way, the exhibition functioned as a narrative threshold, exploring new forms of mediation, exhibition, and the launch of audiovisual projects.
